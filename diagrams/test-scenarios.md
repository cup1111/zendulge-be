## Which Format Should I Use?

- **Detailed Steps (this document)**: Best for QA testing, training, and detailed walkthroughs
- **Visual Flowcharts**: Best for presentations and explaining logic flow
- **Quick Reference Matrix**: Best for quick lookups and comparisons
- **Scenario Cards**: Best for printing and sharing with stakeholders

See `test-scenarios-visual.md` for alternative formats.
